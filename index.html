About Nefeli

Nefeli is a cryptocurrency designed to be accessible, decentralized, and privacy-focused. Built to empower individuals, Nefeli aims to ensure fair mining opportunities and secure transactions for everyone.

Features

Privacy

Advanced privacy features to keep your transactions secure and anonymous.

Accessibility

Designed for individual miners with CPU-friendly algorithms.

Decentralization

Promoting fairness and preventing dominance by large mining farms.
